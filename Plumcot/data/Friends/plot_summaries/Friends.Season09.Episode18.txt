Hoping to win a huge jackpot, the friends pool their money and buy dozens of lottery tickets. However, they bicker over how to spend the fortune if they win, and Monica irritates everyone else by buying tickets just for her and Chandler.
When everyone except Ross pools together to buy tickets for the lottery, Phoebe gets a prediction from her psychic that they will win. Ross, despite his skepticism, gets in on the action. Meanwhile, Chandler is anxious to find out if he gets a coveted assistant job at his advertising firm, and Emma is due to say her first word.
Everyone except for Ross goes to Connecticut to buy lottery tickets. When everyone is fighting over how much money they each are going to get, Phoebe accidentally drops all the tickets over the balcony. Emma says her first word and Ross thinks it is not a real word, but it is. Chandler waits for a call to get a new better paying job.
