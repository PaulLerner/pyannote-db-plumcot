The gang frantically search for Ross's monkey Marcel after Rachel loses him.
Ross asks Rachel to keep Marcel for a day, and she loses him. Everyone starts looking for him, and Rachel calls Animal Control over to help, but she doesn't know that Ross is not allowed to have an exotic monkey. The Animal Control employee is in fact a former schoolmate of Monica and Rachel, and she hates them, so they all race to find the monkey first.
When Rachel reads her ex Barry is about to marry her friend Mindy, she's amazed to have mixed feelings. Then Barry appears at the apartment. Ross leaves his capucin monkey with Rachel while he gets the right wine for what he hopes will be a romantic evening with her, but distracted Rachel leaves the door open and Marcel disappears. Ross is frantic and everybody goes looking. He is furious to find out Rachel called animal control as Marcel is an illegal exotic pet. The animal control officer turns out to be Louisa, who carries a deep grudge against Monica and Rachel for ignoring her in high-school...
