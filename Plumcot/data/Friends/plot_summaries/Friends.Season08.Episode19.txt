Joey prepares for his epic interview with Soap Opera Digest, reminiscing about his past acting experiences, his friends and his romantic life.
Rachel is trying to solve the crossword in Soap Opera Digest magazine and sees that Joey is the answer to one of the clues. Joey tells his friends that he will be interviewed for an article about "Days of Our Lives". Everyone is very excited about it, but they're worried about Joey and his habit of saying the first thing that comes into his head. However, they promise to be near Joey during the interview, which takes place in the coffee house. There, Joey tells the reporter about his life, while we see some flash-backs of old episodes. But when Joey's about to say something wrong, everyone rushes in and stops him. Then Joey talks about his friends and who could be his best friend.
