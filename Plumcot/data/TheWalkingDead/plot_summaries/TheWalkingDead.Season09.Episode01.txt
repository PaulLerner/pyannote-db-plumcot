Rick and his group make a risky run into Washington, D.C. to search for artifacts they will need to build the civilization he and Carl envisioned.
Alexandria, The Kingdom, Hilltop and The Sanctuary are living peacefully in the post-apocalyptic world. Rick and a group goes to Washington to scavenge seeds, a wagon and tools in a museum but when they are returning, the young Ken is attacked by a walker and dies. His parents blame Maggie for the death of his beloved son and Gregory manipulates the old couple.
