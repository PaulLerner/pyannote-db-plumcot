Supplies go missing from Hilltop; Negan is idolized by Brandon; Ezekiel holds a secret.
While hunting a boar, Kelly has hearing problem and is attacked by walkers and passes out. Negan is hunted by a Hilltop group and he meets Brandon that worships him. In Hilltop, supplies are being stolen. Ezekiel is coughing and Siddiq discovers a secret. Negan meets two survivors. Amelia and her son Milo, and asks Brandon to return to Alexandria. He gets close to the boy and intends to take them both to Hilltop but something tragic happens. Daryl, Connie and Magna find Kelly and the supplies that are missing. Negan goes to the border to meet the Whisperers.
