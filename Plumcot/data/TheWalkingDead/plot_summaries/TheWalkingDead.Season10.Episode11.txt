The Whisperers are coming for Hilltop, after Daryl and Lydia's encounter with Alpha, the communities must decide whether to run or fight; Eugene's communication with Stephanie gets complicated.
At Hilltop, Eugene has a long conversation with Stephanie and proposes to meet her wherever she wants in Virginia and she promises to talk to her fellow people about their encounter. Meanwhile Aiden and Earl are improving their defenses when a group from Alexandria arrives with Mary. Then Daryl and Lydia arrive and disclose that Alpha, the Whisperers and the horde of walkers are coming. Daryl flees with the children but finds that the road is blocked. They decide to stay at Hilltop and fight the enemy.
