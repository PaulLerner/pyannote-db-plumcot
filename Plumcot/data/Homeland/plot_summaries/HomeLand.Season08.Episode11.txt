Claire Danes stars as CIA officer Carrie Mathison, who is top in her field despite being volatile and unpredictable. This final season finds Carrie recovering from months of brutal confinement in a Russian gulag. Her body is healing, but her memory remains fractured - and that is a problem for Saul, now National Security Advisor to the newly ascendant President Warner.
