** THIS SYNOPSIS CONTAINS SPOILERS. **It's Thanksgiving, and everyone is making plans for the holiday, though most of the doctors are working. Abby gives her diseased mother a bus ticket to Florida, but she refuses to leave. She returns to the hospital in a desperate attempt to make amends with her estranged daughter. Meanwhile, Carter misdiagnoses a critically ill patient. Corday is sued for malpractice and receives some shocking news; she's pregnant. Greene's strange headaches and unability to speak is diagnoses with a brain tumor, which will impact his future with Elizabeth. Chen's mother makes a surprise visit to the hospital. A very pregnant Chen tells her something about her grandchild that will affect their entire relationship. An explosion occurs in the hospital, leaving Abby injured and the hospital evacuated.
