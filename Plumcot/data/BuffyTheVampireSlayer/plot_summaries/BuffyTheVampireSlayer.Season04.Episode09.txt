One of Willow's spells causes her statements to become reality, with consequences both entertaining and life-threatening.
Willow has another try at active magic, trying to cure her lovesick longing for Oz by means of a spell which makes her will come true. But she thinks it failed while it's only delayed. So, without realizing it she blinds Giles, switches Amy from rat to girl and back, has Buffy and Spike engaged to be married and makes Xander attract demons. When Anya, Xander and Giles figure out what has happened, they to go find Willow. But D'Hoffryn has summoned Willow, intending to make her a Vengeance Demon. Anya tries to contact D'Hoffryn while everyone else tries to defend against the demons that are attracted to Xander.
