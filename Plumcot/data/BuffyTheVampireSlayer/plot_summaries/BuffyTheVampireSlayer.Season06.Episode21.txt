Fueled by black magic, Willow targets Andrew and Jonathan, leading to a battle between Buffy and Willow; Spike fights an unstoppable foe.
After killing Warren, Dark Willow tries to track-down Andrew and Jonathan to kill them, which prompts Buffy to rescue them from their jail cell and hide them at the Magic Shop thus putting herself in the unlikely role of nerd/villain protector since Buffy is the only one who believes it's wrong to kill humans regardless of how evil or murderous they are. Meanwhile in Africa, Spike battles against the first of several foes to earn the right to have is request granted.
