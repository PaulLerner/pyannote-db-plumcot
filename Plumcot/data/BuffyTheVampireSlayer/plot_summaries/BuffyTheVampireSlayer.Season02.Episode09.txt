Spike brings in three professional bounty hunters to eliminate Buffy while she ponders on what her profession might be if her fate wasn't already sealed.
"Career Week", a must at Sunnydale High by order of the principal, finds Xander has a future as jail guard while Willow and geek Oz are nerdy first choice for top class companies. Buffy realizes that she has practically no future apart from that of a Slayer, at best law enforcer. Meanwhile, Spike sends the order of Taraka, deadly assassins who can be any form, to eliminate the Slayer. Giles and Willow's research a special cross stolen from the tomb of Du Lac, a theologian and mathematician, which is a key to his coded manuscript a vampire minion stole from Giles' library. It apparently contains a cure for Spike's weakened, crazy vampire lover, Drusilla.
