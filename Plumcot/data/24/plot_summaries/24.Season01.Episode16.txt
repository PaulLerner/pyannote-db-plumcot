After handing out photos of all the assassins, Jack and Mason discover that one of Palmer's campaign staff is in a romantic relationship with Alexis Drazen. Teri and Kim are attacked in the safehouse. Keith confronts Carl Webb about the cover-up.
On the day of the California Presidential Primary, between 3:00 PM and 4:00 PM, Bob Ellis is murdered in the toilet of a bar in French Quarter. Nina feels that Kim has feelings for Rick and is hiding information, but she leaves the safe-house since Teri is not feeling comfortable with her. When Elizabeth Nash, from the senator's advanced team, sees the photo of Alexis Drazen, she tells David Palmer about her affair and is sent to CTU for interrogation. She accepts the request of Jack Bauer to plant a bug in Alexis to permit tracing him. Keith schedules a meeting with Carl and records their conversation, when Carl explains why his father has backed off. The terrorists kill the agents and break in the safe-house, but Teri and Kim escape in a car. After an accident, Teri has amnesia due to her trauma.
