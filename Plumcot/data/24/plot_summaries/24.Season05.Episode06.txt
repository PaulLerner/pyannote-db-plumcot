Bauer goes to Mike Novick with evidence implicating the President's chief of staff. Nerve gas arrives at the port: is the danger to L.A. or elsewhere?
Jack Bauer arranges to meet with Mike Novick after discovering that Walt Cummings is involved in the terrorist plot. Pre-empting this, Walt reveals his involvement and the true nature of the plot to President Logan. The nerve gas arrives at the port, ready for its trip to Moscow as CTU continues its investigation, still under the assumption that it is to be used against Americans.
