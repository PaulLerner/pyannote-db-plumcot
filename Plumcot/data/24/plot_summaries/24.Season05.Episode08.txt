Jack poses as a terrorist and hundreds of lives are at risk in a shopping mall. Meanwhile, the President must manage a high-profile suicide.
Posing as Rossler, Jack goes under cover with the terrorists in the hope that they will lead him to Erwich, even at the expense of hundreds of lives in a shopping mall. Meanwhile, President Logan, Martha Logan and Mike Novick debate how best to deal with the suicide of Walt Cummings.
