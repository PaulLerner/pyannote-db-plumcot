Fractions develop within the team as they struggle to decide if Naomi is here to rescue them, or if there is an ulterior motive. There are more jumps to the future, and back again to the island, as we learn more about the fate of Flight Oceanic 815. The arrival of the rest of Naomi's team further fuels debate about whether they can trust them, as Ben begins to let on that he knows more than he is divulging.
